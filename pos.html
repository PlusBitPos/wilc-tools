DISCLAIMER: WILC POS app eliminates the involvement of a third-party processor, allowing merchants to accept payments directly from their customers within seconds, in a secured and trustless environment. No node required, no fees, no middleman, no additional hardware. Unlike custodial processors, WILC POS does not store any funds or key of merchants. It is an entirely P2P payment solution, with the convenience and benefits of a payment processor. Cryptocurrency goes directly to merchant's wallet. This app uses ether-js javascript library for address confirmations and listening incoming transactions. On the other hand Infura is used as provider. There is no monthly fee for merchants. It is completely free app for everyone.
